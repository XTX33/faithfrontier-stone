  <h2>Why Faith Frontier Exists</h2>
  <p>
    Faith Frontier emerged when rumors, political speech, and misread records were treated as fact â€”
    when a tradesman and small-business owner was held for months before a hearing, when wage claims
    and benefits decisions moved faster on paper than in the world, and when conscience had to fight
    to remain awake.
  </p>
  <p>
    This project turns that ordeal outward. It works to reconcile
    <strong>due process, public record, and spiritual honesty</strong> â€” revealing what occurs
    not only in opinions and press releases, but in the filings, orders, and delays that contour
    an ordinary life.
  </p>
  <p>
